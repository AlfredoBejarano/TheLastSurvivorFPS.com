Tabs

Here you can download the tabs I made for every song. The tabs are in Guitar Pro (.gp5) format and special software is required in order to play them, such as Guitar Pro, GoPlayAlong Tab Player or TuxGuitar.

While recording a song I usually come up with new arrangements and ideas that I usually forget to add in the tablatures. If you find any mistake, please leave a comment in it respective YouTube video.
